
# NSD – Neighborhood Safety Detector

NSD is a community-based safety and suspicious activity detection platform.

The goal of NSD is to improve safety in communities, schools, and public spaces
by combining IoT devices, GPS tracking, reporting systems, and AI-based detection.

## Purpose
- Community safety
- Anti-stalking protection
- School child safety
- Elderly monitoring
- Incident reporting platform
